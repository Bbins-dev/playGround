Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Binbox Games ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our apps, including Stick It!.

By using our apps, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • General location data (based on IP address)

You can reset or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Cloud Save Data

To sync your game progress across devices, we use platform-provided cloud services:

  • iOS: Apple iCloud Key-Value Storage — syncs level progress and purchase status
  • Android: Google Play Games Services — syncs level progress via OAuth-authenticated save data

This data is stored and managed by Apple and Google respectively, under their own privacy policies.

Purchase Data

In-app purchases (such as "Remove Ads") are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We receive a purchase receipt to verify the transaction but do not collect or store any payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.).

Local Data

Our apps store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Level progress and completion status
  • Sound and display preferences
  • Purchase verification status

This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Device Identifiers

A device-unique identifier is used locally as a cryptographic salt for data integrity checks (tamper detection). This identifier is never transmitted to any server.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location. We do not use analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics or Unity Analytics), and we do not collect crash reports.

3. How We Use Information

  • Display advertisements: AdMob uses device and advertising data to serve ads
  • Sync game progress: Cloud save services store your level completion data across devices
  • Process purchases: Purchase receipts verify your in-app purchases
  • Ensure data integrity: Device identifiers are used locally to detect data tampering

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps integrate the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Apple iCloud — cloud save (iOS)
  • Google Play Games Services — cloud save (Android)
  • Apple App Store — in-app purchases (iOS)
  • Google Play Store — in-app purchases (Android)

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Cloud save data: Retained by Apple iCloud or Google Play Games according to their respective data retention policies
  • Advertising data: Managed by Google AdMob under Google's data retention policies

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete local data: Uninstall the app or clear app data through your device settings
  • Delete cloud save data: Manage through your iCloud account settings (iOS) or Google Play Games settings (Android)
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Adjust ad personalization in your device settings
  • Reset advertising identifier: Reset your AAID or IDFA through device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
